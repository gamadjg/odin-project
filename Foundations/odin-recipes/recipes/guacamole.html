Guacamole

Recipe Description

Guacamole recipe so you can tell people that you know how to make it...

Ingredients

  • 2 Avocados
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 ripe tomato, chopped
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Peel avocados and mash in a serving bowl
  2. Stir onion, garlic, tomato, lime juice, salt and pepper
