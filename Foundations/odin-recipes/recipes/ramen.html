Ramen

Recipe Description

Description...

Ingredients

  • 3.5 cups, Vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5oz) package, ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

Steps

  1. Add broth and noodles within a saucepan
  2. Boil noodles and broth
  3. Stir and break up noodles
  4. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, Chili oil, and ginger
  5. Simmer for 10 minutes
  6. Add sesame oil and green onions
